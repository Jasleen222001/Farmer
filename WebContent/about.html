ABOUT CROP LOGS

CROP-LOGS is a website developed for reducing the workload of farmers in maintaining their records manually and apart from that this website guides farmers about latest trends, pricing and weather updates through You-Tube links, so that they can get all relevant information at one place..

Reducing manual effort, here farmers can store their crop records and can analyse their profit or loss based on their yield in previous years. The data is stored in Database(MongoDB) where user can fetch their data anytime and can also update and delete their records accordingly.

Most importantly, Data stored in Database increases its safety and user dosen't have to maintain manual records(which can easily be destroyed and security is minimum) whereas in this website user can access the record only through his login credentials and hence make it more reliable.
